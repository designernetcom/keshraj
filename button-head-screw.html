Button Head Screw

A button head screw is a type of threaded fastener characterized by its low-profile, rounded, and slightly domed head with a socket drive. It is similar to a cap screw but with a lower-profile head design, making it ideal for applications where a flat or low-profile head is desired to reduce snagging or provide a finished appearance.

Button head screws are often used in applications where a flush or low-profile surface is desired, such as in machine parts, electronics, automotive components, and furniture assembly.The low-profile head minimizes protrusion and provides a clean, finished appearance.

The head of a button head screw is rounded and typically cylindrical with a slight dome or curve on the top. The design provides a smooth and low-profile surface when installed, making it less likely to snag on surrounding objects or materials.
